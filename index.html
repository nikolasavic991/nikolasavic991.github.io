General Privacy Policy version 1.1

Last updated: 07.09.2018.

Demolition Hammer Interactive uses advertisements to monetize its android applications. In order to serve advertisements we or our partners collect some personalized information about its users. Please note that our partners operate under their own privacy policies

What information we or our partners collect

• Advertising ID : The advertising ID is a unique, user-resettable ID for advertising, provided by Google Play services. It gives users better controls and provides developers with a simple, standard system to continue to monetize their apps. It enables users to reset their identifier or opt out of personalized ads (formerly known as interest-based ads) within Google Play apps.

• Information that is passively collected by Unity: Our applications are proudly powered by Unity3D technology and incorporate Unity Ads for advertisements delivery. Information that is passively collected by Unity may or may not include some or all of folowing: Unique device identifier generated from the device MAC/IMEI/MEID, an advertising ID, such as the Apple IDFA or the Android Ad ID, IP address,device manufacturer and model, the operating system and version running on your system or device, browser type,language,the make of the CPU, and number of CPUs present, the graphics card information, such as type, vendor and driver name, version and graphics API,amount of system and video RAM present,current screen resolution,Unity Player and Unity Editor versions,operating system identifier (e.g., Mac, Windows, etc.),a checksum of all the data that gets sent to verify that it did transmit correctly and App ID of the game installed. Additionally, Unity Ads may collect some other information in order to deliver personalized advertisements.

How is this information used:

• Advertising ID is being collected and used by our ad network partner AdMob. For details on how this information is used by our partner AdMob please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en

• Information that is passively collected by Unity is being collected and used by our partner Unity. For details on how this information is used by our partner Unity please visit: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy
